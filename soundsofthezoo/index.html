Today is a good day to be out and about,

Avery couldn’t help but let out a shout.

It’s her first time to the Taronga zoo

She’s excited to hear what animals can do.

From afar she could already hear the elephant’s TOOT!

Jumping for joy, she ran inside in a scoot.

A short little trip to Wild Africa,

And there she saw the striped mighty fella.

Standing strong and broad in a deep gaze,

Avery stared back in awe and amaze.

To her surprise the majestic tiger let out a loud ROAR!

She covered her ears while the crowd clapped galore.

It’s time to get wet, she headed to the seal show

Front row seat where the water was just a feet away or so.

Sally the Seal put her best flipper forward.

If she did her tricks well she’ll be given a reward.

She slid, she dove and even went through an arc.

At the end of the show she said thank you with a long BARK!

One thing that’s a must see in Sydney,

Is a native Kookaburra who LAUGHS really funny.

Tell a joke and they’ll be at it for hours.

Avery told them the one about the green flowers.

They thought it was hilarious, couldn’t stop their laughter.

Even some of the passerby joined in the chatter.

Last but not the least was the Petting Zoo.

The trip was nearing to an end, Avery was sad too.

Long legs, long ears and a long neck.

White fluffy fur she went for a hug and gave it a peck!

The Alpaca HUMMED and she fell in love right away.

They took a picture to forever remember this day.
